# üíÄ Anti Shiny Sprites

**Anti-shinies are not official, they are simply a fan made concept from the Pok√©Bot stream.**

Pok√©mon that are anti-shiny are the complete opposite of a shiny. Normal shinies have a shiny value (SV) of `0` - `7` (the 8 lowest SV values), while anything with a SV of `65,528` - `65,535` (the 8 highest SV values) is anti-shiny.

The following sprites were generated by inverting their regular shiny sprites with a script.
